About Raju Ventures

Building Trust Through Every Plot We Sell

Who We Are

Raju Ventures is a family-run real-estate business based in Ramachandrapuram, Andhra Pradesh. For over a decade, we have been helping customers find residential, commercial, and farm lands that fit their budget and dream locations.

We specialize in verified open plots and legally approved ventures, ensuring transparency, fair pricing, and complete satisfaction for every client.

Our Mission

To make land investment simple, safe, and profitable for everyone in Andhra Pradesh. We aim to provide clear documentation, fair guidance, and honest service.
